About Me

Welcome to my world of innovation! I'm Maiteth Jones, and I've worn many hats throughout my career. In the past, I worked as a financial analyst, crunching numbers and navigating the world of investments. But there was always another passion simmering beneath the surface - web design.

During my days as a financial analyst, web design was more than just a hobby; it was a creative outlet that allowed me to explore new ideas and push the boundaries of my own skills. However, life's beautiful chaos took over as I started a family, and my web design dreams were temporarily put on hold.

Now, with my children a bit more independent and free time on my side, I've decided to take the plunge into the exciting world of front-end development. I'm embarking on a journey to retrain and explore the limitless possibilities of web innovation.

Join me on this exhilarating ride as I combine my financial acumen with my newfound love for front-end development. Together, we'll navigate the ever-evolving landscape of web design and discover the countless ways innovation can shape our digital world.

Let's create, innovate, and embrace the future together!
